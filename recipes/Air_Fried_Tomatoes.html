Air-Fried Italian Stuffed Tomatoes

Description

Air-Fried Italian Stuffed Tomatoes are a delightful and healthier twist on a classic Italian dish. Ripe, firm tomatoes are hollowed out to create a cavity for a savory filling, which typically includes a mix of Italian-inspired ingredients.

The filling often features a combination of breadcrumbs, garlic, onions, and herbs like basil, oregano, and parsley. For added richness, grated Parmesan or mozzarella cheese is mixed in, along with optional ingredients like ground meat (such as beef or Italian sausage), sautéed spinach, or finely chopped vegetables like bell peppers or zucchini. Some variations include cooked rice or quinoa for a heartier texture.

Once stuffed, the tomatoes are placed in the air fryer, where they cook at a high temperature. The air frying process makes the tomatoes tender while keeping their shape, and the filling becomes golden and slightly crispy on top, with the cheese melting into a gooey, flavorful layer.

The result is a juicy, flavorful tomato with a perfectly cooked, aromatic filling. These Air-Fried Italian Stuffed Tomatoes are often garnished with fresh basil leaves, a sprinkle of additional Parmesan, and a drizzle of olive oil or balsamic glaze. They can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or light main course, offering a delicious taste of Italy with a modern, healthy twist.

ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • olive oil, as needed
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • ⅓ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup crumbled goat cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped toasted walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided, or more to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Direction

  1. Step 1

    Cut tops off tomatoes and scoop out flesh with a melon baller, leaving 1/4- to 1/2-inch thick sides and bottoms. Discard tomato tops and flesh.

  2. Step 2

    Brush the bottom of an air fryer basket with olive oil.

  3. Step 3

    Combine cooked rice, Parmesan cheese, goat cheese, walnuts, 1 tablespoon basil, and garlic in a medium bowl. Combine bread crumbs, and 1 tablespoon olive oil in a small bowl. Fill tomatoes with rice mixture, then sprinkle with bread crumb mixture. Place stuffed tomatoes into the prepared air fryer basket.

  4. Step 4

    Cook in the air fryer at 370 degrees F (188 degrees C) until tomatoes are tender, filling is heated through, and topping is golden brown, about 15 minutes. Garnish with remaining basil.
